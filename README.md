# 🎓 SIH25094 – AI-Powered Student Learning Assistant and Personalized Career & Education Advisor

## 📌 Overview

The AI-Powered Student Learning Assistant and Personalized Career & Education Advisor is an intelligent platform that helps students make informed academic and career decisions. It uses Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, NLP, and Generative AI to provide personalized learning recommendations, career guidance, skill-gap analysis, course suggestions, and academic support through an AI chatbot.

The platform acts as a virtual mentor, guiding students throughout their educational journey and helping them achieve their academic and career goals with confidence.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To empower students with intelligent educational support and personalized career planning through Artificial Intelligence.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📚 Personalized Learning Recommendations
- Customized study plans based on student interests and performance.
- Adaptive learning paths for continuous improvement.

### 🎯 AI-Based Career Guidance
- Career recommendations based on skills, interests, and academic background.
- Insights into future career opportunities and industry trends.

### 📊 Skill Gap Analysis
- Identifies missing skills required for desired career paths.
- Suggests courses and certifications for improvement.

### 🏫 Course & College Recommendations
- Recommends suitable degree programs and colleges.
- Helps students explore educational opportunities.

### 🤖 AI Chatbot for Academic Support
- Provides instant answers to academic and career-related queries.
- Offers personalized guidance and support.

### 📈 Progress Tracking Dashboard
- Monitors learning progress and performance.
- Visualizes achievements and learning milestones.

### 🎓 Scholarship & Entrance Exam Guidance
- Suggests relevant scholarships.
- Provides entrance exam recommendations and preparation guidance.

### 🔄 Adaptive Learning Paths
- Continuously updates recommendations based on student progress.

## 🔍 Workflow

1. Student registers and creates a profile.
2. Academic details, interests, and goals are collected.
3. AI analyzes the student's profile.
4. Personalized learning recommendations are generated.
5. Career guidance and skill-gap analysis are provided.
6. Relevant courses, colleges, and scholarships are suggested.
7. Progress is tracked and recommendations are updated dynamically.
